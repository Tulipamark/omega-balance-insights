# ZZ Growth Signals

## What Actually Matters

### Not important
- total signups
- traffic alone
- passive partners

### Important
- active partners
- customer retention
- duplication
- first-line performance

## Behavioral Interpretation

### Personal production
You can sell.

### Recruitment
You can attract.

### Activation
You can onboard.

### Duplication
You can scale.

### Leadership
You can sustain.

## Core Insight

The compensation plan rewards:

Not:
- individual effort alone

But:
- system behavior

## Translation To Product

We should measure:
- actions
- consistency
- partner activation

We should not measure:
- theoretical payouts
- exact rank
