# Partner First 30 Days

## Purpose

This document defines the first practical version of the partner journey.

It is not a full onboarding academy.
It is not a copy of Zinzino Back Office.

It is a simple operating path that helps a new partner go from:

- signed up
- to active
- to first result
- to early duplication signals

The point is speed, clarity, and repeatability.

---

## Product role

Omega Balance should help a new partner:

- understand what matters first
- avoid doing ten things at once
- get to a first real result quickly
- start first-line activity early

The platform should not try to replace:

- Zinzino qualification logic
- official compensation logic
- full education stacks

It should provide a clear progress path and next-best-action guidance.

---

## Core principle

The first 30 days are not about building everything.

They are about creating early movement in the right order:

1. become active
2. get the first customer
3. get the first partner lead or recruit
4. help first line move

If that works, the partner has a real chance to continue.

---

## What the first 30 days should optimize for

Primary outcomes:

- first customer
- first partner lead
- first recruited partner
- first active first-line signal

Secondary outcomes:

- repeated activity
- consistency
- confidence in the process

Not primary outcomes yet:

- payout estimates
- exact ZZ rank progression
- advanced team structure
- retention optimization

---

## Milestones

### Day 0-2: Get started

Goal:
- understand the model
- get the referral link
- know what first result means

What the partner needs:
- referral link
- short explanation of the journey
- one clear first action

System support:
- show referral link
- show "what to do first"
- show that the first milestone is first customer or first partner lead

---

### Day 3-7: First result

Goal:
- create the first real signal

Accepted first results:
- first customer lead
- first qualified customer
- first partner lead

System support:
- show current status
- show next milestone
- reinforce one narrow focus

Interpretation:
- the partner has started moving
- but should not be treated as stable yet

---

### Day 8-14: Repeat the first success

Goal:
- avoid stopping after one result
- create repeated activity

Signals:
- second customer signal
- second partner signal
- continued lead flow

System support:
- show progress toward `active -> growing`
- show "do this again" rather than adding new complexity

Interpretation:
- this is where many partners either continue or stall

---

### Day 15-21: Start first-line activation

Goal:
- move beyond personal effort

Signals:
- first recruited partner
- first first-line lead
- first first-line customer signal

System support:
- shift guidance from personal output to helping first line act
- show early duplication signals clearly

Interpretation:
- this is the first real transition from solo effort to system behavior

---

### Day 22-30: Early duplication check

Goal:
- identify whether activity is still only personal
- or starting to repeat through others

Signals:
- active first-line partner
- partner-generated lead
- partner-generated customer signal

System support:
- make the gap visible
- explain whether the partner is still building personally or starting to duplicate

Interpretation:
- this is where Growth Compass becomes especially useful

---

## Mapping to Growth Compass

### `inactive`

Likely meaning in first 30 days:
- no real movement yet

Primary message:
- get the first result

### `active`

Likely meaning in first 30 days:
- first movement exists
- not yet repeated

Primary message:
- repeat the first success

### `growing`

Likely meaning in first 30 days:
- real activity exists
- consistency has started
- first-line signals may be emerging

Primary message:
- stop doing everything alone

### `duplicating`

Likely meaning in first 30 days:
- uncommon but very strong early outcome

Primary message:
- protect and repeat what is working through others

### `leader-track`

Likely meaning in first 30 days:
- exceptional early signal
- should be treated carefully and verified over time

Primary message:
- stabilize, do not over-celebrate a spike

## What the partner dashboard should eventually show

The partner view should become a simple action dashboard.

Recommended first version:

- current status
- next milestone
- next best action
- referral link
- personal leads
- partner leads
- direct partners

Recommended second version:

- first 30 days progress
- simple checklist
- first-line activation signal
- duplication signal

---

## What admin should use this for

Admin should use this journey to:

- identify who is moving
- identify who stalled after one result
- identify who is starting first-line behavior
- identify who may need direct support

Admin should not use it as:

- payout truth
- official qualification truth
- a perfect scorecard

---

## Guardrails

- Keep the first 30 days simple.
- Favor repeatable action over inspiration-heavy complexity.
- Do not overload a new partner with too many paths.
- Prefer one next action over five ideas.
- Use Growth Compass as guidance, not as a label machine.

---

## Success criteria

This layer is successful if:

- a new partner understands what matters first
- the first result happens faster
- more partners get a second result
- more partners reach first-line activity
- admin can see who needs help without guessing
