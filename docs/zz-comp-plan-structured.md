# ZZ Compensation Plan – Structured

## Core Drivers

1. Personal activity
   - number of customers
   - repeat purchases

2. Recruitment
   - new partners
   - onboarding success

3. First-line activation
   - active partners in level 1

4. Duplication
   - activity generated by partners

## Growth Signals

### Early stage
- first customer
- first partner

### Build stage
- 2-3 customers
- 1-2 partners

### Activation stage
- first active partner
- repeated activity

### Duplication stage
- multiple active partners
- partner-generated leads

### Leadership stage
- stable structure
- consistent team activity

## What Drives Progression

- consistency > spikes
- active partners > total partners
- partner-generated volume > personal volume
